Every Orbitz flavor has a story—a cosmic tale of inspiration, experimentation, and perfection. Learn how we brought these tastes to life.

The Making of Galactic Grape

Inspired by the swirling purples of distant nebulae, Galactic Grape blends premium grape extracts with a secret touch of sweetness. Our flavor scientists spent months refining this recipe, testing over 50 variations to achieve the perfect balance of bold fruitiness and smooth refreshment. It’s a drink that’s as deep and mysterious as the cosmos itself.

Stellar Citrus Unveiled

Born from the brightest stars in the night sky, Stellar Citrus combines hand-picked oranges, lemons, and a splash of lime for a tangy explosion. We sourced our citrus from the sunniest orchards, ensuring each bottle delivers a vibrant, zesty punch that awakens your senses like a supernova bursting into life.

Meteor Mint’s Cool Secret

Crafted with natural mint leaves grown in pristine mountain regions, Meteor Mint offers a chilling sensation that’s both crisp and invigorating. We experimented with different mint varieties to find the one that delivers the ultimate cool-down, making it the perfect refresher for any time of day.

Orbitz Cocktail Recipes

Turn your Orbitz into a stellar cocktail with these easy-to-make recipes. Perfect for parties or a relaxing evening at home!

Galactic Grape Spritzer

Ingredients: Galactic Grape Orbitz, sparkling water, fresh grapes, ice.
Instructions: Fill a glass with ice, pour in Galactic Grape Orbitz, top with sparkling water, and garnish with fresh grapes. Stir gently and enjoy!

Citrus Star Martini

Ingredients: Stellar Citrus Orbitz, vodka, lemon juice, ice.
Instructions: Shake Stellar Citrus Orbitz, vodka, and lemon juice with ice. Strain into a martini glass and garnish with a lemon twist.

Mint Meteor Mojito

Ingredients: Meteor Mint Orbitz, rum, fresh mint, lime juice, ice.
Instructions: Muddle mint and lime juice, add ice, rum, and Meteor Mint Orbitz. Stir well and garnish with a mint sprig.
